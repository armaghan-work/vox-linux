# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

This project is open and welcoming to everyone. We do not tolerate harassment or
exclusionary behaviour of any kind.

## Expected behaviour

- Be respectful and constructive in issues, PRs, and discussions
- Focus on the work, not the person
- Accept feedback graciously

## Unacceptable behaviour

- Personal attacks, insults, or derogatory comments
- Spam or off-topic content

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behaviour, open a private issue or
contact the maintainer directly via GitHub.

## Enforcement

The maintainer reserves the right to remove comments, close issues, or ban
users who violate this code of conduct.
